How It Works

Two sides.
One marketplace.

Whether you're earning or growing, the process is simple, transparent, and built on proof.

👷 For Workers Earn Money
1
Sign up for free
Create your Worker account in under a minute. No fees, no upfront cost.
2
Browse available tasks
Pick tasks that suit you — social media, reviews, app downloads, and more.
3
Complete and submit proof
Do the task, upload your screenshot or paste your proof text.
4
Get paid to your wallet
Advertiser reviews your proof and credits your wallet. Withdraw anytime.
📢 For Advertisers Grow Your Brand
1
Create your account
Sign up and choose the Advertiser role, or switch to it anytime.
2
Deposit funds
Transfer to our account and submit proof. Admin credits your wallet.
3
Post your task
Set reward per slot, number of slots, description, and proof type required.
4
Review and approve proofs
Workers submit proof, you review it. Approve to pay them, reject if invalid.
Platform Features

Built for trust.
Designed for scale.

Every feature exists to protect both sides — workers get paid fairly, advertisers get real results.

💰
Wallet System

Every naira is tracked in real time. Available, reserved, and pending balances always shown clearly. Full transaction ledger included.

📸
Proof-Based Payments

Workers submit screenshots, text, or both as proof. Advertisers verify before any payment is released. No proof, no pay.

🔒
One Submission Per Task

Each worker can only submit once per task. No duplicate submissions, no gaming the system.

🔄
Flexible Roles

Start as a Worker and switch to Advertiser anytime — or vice versa. Your wallet balance carries over. One account, both worlds.

📒
Immutable Ledger

Every transaction is permanently recorded. No record can be altered or deleted. Complete financial transparency for every account.

🛡️
Admin Oversight

All deposits and withdrawals go through admin approval. Disputes are handled manually. Platform integrity protected at every step.
