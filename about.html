About CunkPay

Built for people who have time.
Built for brands that need reach.

CunkPay started with a simple idea — there are people with free time willing to do small online tasks, and there are businesses that desperately need real human engagement on their content. We built the bridge.

"We believe your free time has value. Whether you have 30 minutes or 3 hours, there's a task waiting for you — and a payment waiting when you're done."

CunkPay is a micro-task marketplace that connects two types of people: workers who want to earn money in their spare time, and advertisers who want real human interactions on their websites, apps, social media accounts, and more. The platform handles the money, the proof, and the trust layer — so both sides can focus on what they do best.

What we stand for

🔍
Transparency

Every fee is visible before you act. Deposit fees, withdrawal fees, and task budgets are all shown clearly. No surprises, no hidden charges.

🤝
Fairness

Workers get paid for approved work. Advertisers get real proof of task completion. Neither side pays more than they agreed to.

🔒
Trust

An immutable transaction ledger means every naira is accounted for. Admin oversight ensures deposits and withdrawals are verified before processing.

⚡
Simplicity

Sign up, choose a task, submit proof, get paid. Post a task, fund it, review proofs, grow your brand. CunkPay removes every unnecessary step.
